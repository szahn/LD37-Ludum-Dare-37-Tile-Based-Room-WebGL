Ludum Dare 37: One Room

Use the arrow keys to navigate around the room.
Avoid getting stuck on walls.
Some walls move up and down in a repeated fashion.
There is a gem you must find somewhere hidden by the walls that mock you.
If you get stuck, just press F5 to restart the game.

Note: This is a poorly optimized WebGL game, so gliches may occur. It's highly recommended you play this in the Google Chrome browser.
